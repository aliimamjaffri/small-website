Pancake Recipe

Ingredients for Pancakes

  • 1 cup of flour
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon of salt
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons of melted butter

Steps to Make Pancakes

  1. Mix the dry ingredients together.
  2. Whisk in the milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Heat a skillet and pour ¼ cup of butter for each pancake.
  4. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown.

Glossary of Cooking Terms

Saute
Cook quick in a small amount of oil over high heat.
Bake
Cook food in an oven using dry heat.
Simmer
Cook gently just below boiling point.
